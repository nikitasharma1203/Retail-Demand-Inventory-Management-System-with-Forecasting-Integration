## Suggested Slide Structure (12 slides)

1. **Title Slide** — Project name, team, course
2. **Problem Statement** — Why retail sales forecasting matters
3. **Dataset Overview** — sales.csv, stores.csv, features.csv
4. **ER Diagram** — Entity-Relationship diagram (see ER_Diagram.png)
5. **Relational Schema** — DDL tables with constraints
6. **Triggers & Procedures** — Business logic automation
7. **Query Highlights** — Key SQL queries with results
8. **Views** — Reusable analytical views
9. **Data Quality Results** — Orphan detection output
10. **Dashboard Demo** — Streamlit screenshots
11. **Demand Forecasting** — SARIMA / Prophet results
12. **Conclusion & Extensions** — Role-based access, future work
